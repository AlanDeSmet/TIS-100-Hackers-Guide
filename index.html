TIS-100 Hacker's Guide

Introduction

The TIS-100 Hacker's Guide is for people intrested in the guts of TIS-100.

TIS-100 Puzzle Lua documents how TIS-100 interacts with puzzles written in Lua, for use both by puzzles creators and emulator creators.

TIS-100 Assembly is designed to carefully document the exact behavior of TIS-100 solutions, with an eye towards supporting emulators.

TIS-100 Save File Format documents how TIS-100 saves solutions on disk. This may be useful to emulators wishing to load or create compatible save games.
